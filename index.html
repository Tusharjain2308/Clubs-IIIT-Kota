CREATIVE ARTS AND CULTURAL SOCIETY

Presiding over all the clubs of the college, IIIT KOTA

The Creative Arts and Cultural Society (CACS) at IIIT Kota is a vibrant and dynamic organization that plays a pivotal role in fostering creativity, artistic expression, and cultural appreciation among the students. Founded with the aim of promoting and preserving the rich cultural heritage of India, CACS provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and indulge in various art forms.
